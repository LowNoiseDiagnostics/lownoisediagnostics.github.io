### Low-Noise Diagnostics

*Structural clarity for complex organisational environments.*

Low-Noise Diagnostics produces concise, model-assisted, human-curated structural analyses.
Working on drift, friction, alignment and decision architecture — without coaching, therapy, or strategic prescription.

Outputs are designed as **quiet, high-signal diagnostics** for teams, NGOs and institutions operating under complexity.

---

### Services

**Organizational Drift Diagnostic**  
Missed deliverables, unclear priorities, parallel work streams.

**Decision-Making Breakdown Diagnostic**  
Decisions duplicated, escalated, or stalled; accountability gaps.

**Cross-Team Collaboration Diagnostic**  
Information hoarding, shadow processes, interface friction.

**Strategic Load Assessment**  
Burnout indicators, capacity–commitment mismatch, overload signals.

**Leadership Alignment Diagnostic**  
Conflicting narratives, competing priorities, trust erosion.

**Exploratory Slot**  
Case-dependent deep-dive.

---

### Scope  
Structural diagnostic only. No coaching, implementation, HR or legal advice.

### Workflow  
Asynchronous, low-interaction, written diagnostics only.
